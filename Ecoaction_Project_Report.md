   Tier 1 (Samar hospital + Lychkove water tower) are the strongest — full engineering docs, clear costs, real community need. Recommend starting there.

2. **Do we wait for Friday's batch?**
   ~20 more projects coming from Ecoaction's competition. Could load Tier 1 now and batch the rest, or wait and do one big load.

3. **How to handle relocated communities?**
   These are compelling stories — occupied towns rebuilding in host communities — but the site currently shows projects by municipality location. Need to decide how to present "Pokrovsk → Zakarpattia" on a map.

4. **What's the cost ceiling?**
   Tier 3 projects range from $1.2M to $14.6M. Are these realistic for individual donor funding, or should they be set aside for institutional/government funding?

5. **Non-energy projects?**
   IDP housing (€2.85M), computers (3.3M UAH), and the feasibility study don't fit the current energy/infrastructure focus. Include or filter?

   The 66 existing projects need to come off the site. Timing — before or simultaneously with new project upload?
