### Page 2 — Trust & Transparency

**Verification Process**
1. NGO Partner Verification — every project submitted or verified by a trusted NGO partner with established presence in Ukraine
2. Project Documentation — cost estimates, engineering assessments, and municipal authorizations required before listing
3. Banking Verification — municipalities provide verified banking details for direct wire transfers, all tracked and confirmed
4. OFAC Compliance — all municipalities and individuals screened against OFAC sanctions lists before any funds transferred

**Where Your Support Goes**
- Donation processed through POCACITO Network → transferred to municipality for the project they requested
- You always know exactly which community and project your contribution supports — from request through funding to completion

**Procurement Transparency**
- All municipal procurement goes through Prozorro, Ukraine's mandatory public e-procurement platform
- Every purchase is publicly auditable
- NGO partners share on-site photos during installation + live monitoring data once systems operational

**Donor Experience**
- Browse projects with full cost breakdowns — no black boxes
- Tax-deductible donation (501(c)(3))
- Donor portal with project timeline, status updates, photos
- Tax receipt generated automatically

**FAQ Highlights**
- "Projects are funded in full by a single donor — that's how we guarantee completion. Some projects have co-financing partners covering up to 20%."
- "We don't publish exact site coordinates, and access is geoblocked in Russia and Belarus."
