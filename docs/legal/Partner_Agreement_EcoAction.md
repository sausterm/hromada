# PARTNERSHIP AGREEMENT

## Between Hromada and EcoAction Ukraine

**Effective Date:** ___________________________________

---

## 1. Parties

**Hromada** ("Hromada"), a platform that connects American donors with Ukrainian municipalities for civilian infrastructure rebuilding, operated by Thomas Protzman.

**EcoAction Ukraine** ("Partner"), a Ukrainian non-governmental organization registered under the laws of Ukraine.

---

## 2. Purpose

This Agreement establishes a partnership under which the Partner's infrastructure projects and organizational profile are featured on the Hromada platform (hromadaproject.org). At this stage, Hromada operates as a project directory — no funds are solicited, collected, or disbursed through the platform under this Agreement.

---

## 3. What the Partner Provides

The Partner agrees to:

a. Provide project descriptions, photographs, cost estimates, and other materials ("Partner Content") for display on the Hromada platform.

b. Grant Hromada a non-exclusive, royalty-free license to use, display, and distribute Partner Content on the Hromada platform, in social media, and in materials related to Hromada's mission. The Partner represents that it has the right to grant this license, including any necessary consents from individuals depicted in photographs or videos.

c. Notify Hromada if any project information becomes materially inaccurate or if a project is no longer active.

---

## 4. What Hromada Provides

Hromada agrees to:

a. List the Partner as an official partner on the Hromada platform, including display of the Partner's name and logo.

b. Attribute the Partner's role on each project detail page where the Partner is involved.

c. Not publicly misrepresent the Partner's role, capabilities, or endorsement.

d. Remove or update any Partner Content within a reasonable time upon the Partner's written request.

---

## 5. Logo and Brand Usage

a. Each party may use the other party's name and logo solely in connection with this partnership and the Hromada platform. Any other use requires prior written approval.

b. Neither party shall represent itself as an agent, employee, or official representative of the other.

---

## 6. No Financial Obligations

This Agreement does not create any financial obligation between the parties. No funds are exchanged, held, or disbursed under this Agreement. If Hromada later begins accepting or disbursing donations, a separate agreement governing fund flow, compliance, and reporting will be executed before any funds are associated with the Partner's projects.

---

## 7. Term and Termination

a. This Agreement shall take effect on the date written above and shall remain in effect until terminated by either party.

b. Either party may terminate this Agreement at any time by providing thirty (30) days' written notice to the other party.

c. Upon termination, Hromada shall remove the Partner's logo and project listings from the platform within a reasonable time. The content license granted in Section 3(b) shall survive termination with respect to Partner Content already published prior to termination, unless the Partner requests removal for privacy or legal reasons.

---

## 8. Future Agreements

The parties anticipate entering into a more comprehensive Memorandum of Understanding governing fund disbursement, compliance, monitoring, and reporting obligations at a later date. This Agreement shall remain in effect until superseded by such an agreement or terminated under Section 7.
